How to Apply for a Visa

Follow these steps for successful visa application

1

Determine the type of visa

Identify the correct visa type based on your purpose of travel (tourism, business, study, etc.).

2

Gather required documents

Prepare all necessary documents including passport (valid for at least 6 months), photos, application form, financial proofs, and supporting documents.

3

Submit your application

Submit your complete application to the respective embassy, consulate, or authorized visa application center.

4

Pay fees and schedule interview

Pay the visa application fee and schedule an interview if required by the embassy.

5

Wait for processing

Processing times vary by country and visa type. Track your application status online.

General Visa Requirements

While requirements vary by country, these are common documents needed for most visa applications:

  • Valid passport (minimum 6 months validity)
  • Completed visa application form
  • Passport-sized photographs
  • Proof of travel arrangements
  • Proof of accommodation
  • Financial proof (bank statements)
  • Travel insurance (for Schengen visas)
